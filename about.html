About

I am a hands-on engineering leader, software engineer and tech enthusiast living in Prague. I am interested in all things web and love to explore and learn new things.

I grew up in a small town in the Czech Republic. Having been always passionate about technology and especially the internet, I ended up studying Information Technology at Brno University of Technology.

I've lived in London for about seven years and that's where I've learned the most. Absolutely love the city.

After about seven years in London I moved to Prague and have been enjoying the city ever since. My background is fullstack development, engineering leadership, infrastructure and product delivery.

When I'm not working you can usually find me in the closest crossfit box, playing badminton or conquering Spanish. When it's beer o'clock, I'm happy to hit the local pub for a nice pint of craft beer. And finally, when I manage to get some more time off, I just love travelling!
