About

I am a Software Engineer and tech enthusiast living in London. I am interested in all things web and love to explore and learn new things.

I grew up in Dobruška, a small town in the Czech Republic. Having been always passionate about technology and especially the internet, I ended up studying Information Technology at Brno University of Technology.

About four years ago I moved to London and have been enjoying the city ever since. I have always worked as a Fullstack Developer and currently I am working at Adthena and prototyping a few ideas of my own.

When I'm not working you can usually find my in the closest gym, playing squash or conquering Spanish. When it's beer o'clock, I'm happy to hit the local pub for a nice pint of craft beer. And finally, when I manage to get some more time off, I just love travelling!
